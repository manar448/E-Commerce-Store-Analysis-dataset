# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

This project presents an **interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as sales, profit, customers, and product categories.

The dashboard provides insights into:

* Sales distribution by state and city
* Profit trends over time
* Customer purchasing behavior
* Payment method preferences
* Category and subcategory performance


## 🚀 Features

* 📍 **Amount by State**
  Visualizes total sales across different states.

* 💳 **Quantity by Payment Method**
  Displays distribution of orders across:

  * COD
  * Credit Card
  * Debit Card
  * EMI
  * UPI

* 📦 **Profit by Subcategory**
  Identifies the most and least profitable product subcategories.

* 🏙️ **Orders by City**
  Highlights cities with the highest number of orders.

* 📅 **Profit by Month**
  Shows monthly profit trends to identify seasonality and performance dips.

* 👥 **Amount by Customer**
  Displays top customers based on total purchase value.

* 🧾 **Quantity by Category**
  Compares sales across major product categories:

  * Clothing
  * Electronics
  * Furniture

* 📌 **KPIs (Key Metrics)**

  * **Total Profit:** 36,963
  * **Total Quantity Sold:** 5,615
  * **Total Sales Amount:** 43,771

## 💡 Insights

* COD is the most preferred payment method.
* Certain subcategories generate significantly higher profit than others.
* Profit shows fluctuations across months, with peaks in later months.
* A few customers contribute a large portion of total revenue.
* Clothing dominates overall sales quantity.

   ```bash

   ```

2. Open the dashboard file using the appropriate tool:

   * Power BI → `.pbix`

   * Excel → `.xlsx`

3. Explore filters and visuals to analyze data.
